Help & Support

How do I book a session?

You can book a session by logging into your account and selecting an available therapist from the list.

What should I do if I need to cancel?

If you need to cancel, you can do so from your account dashboard. Please try to cancel at least 24 hours in advance.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via the contact form below or by emailing support@therapyplatform.com.
